KAWAII AQUA PETS DEMO CATALOG QUICK START

1. Replace Code.gs in Apps Script.
2. Run setupStore() ONCE.
3. Redeploy Apps Script as a New version.
4. Keep your working Web App URL in config.js.
5. Upload index.html, admin.html, app.js, admin.js, app.css, config.js, and assets to GitHub.
6. Hard refresh.

- 4 categories: Betta, Guppies, Snails, Shrimps
- 20 sample products
- 18 clearly labeled DEMO REVIEW entries
- Clickable category chips
- Local customer-view preview even before config.js is connected
